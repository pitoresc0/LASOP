News

LASOP is a laboratory affiliated with the Department of Chemical Engineering at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul, focused on separation processes and unit operations. It conducts experimental studies and modeling of mass and energy transfer phenomena, with emphasis on adsorption, phase equilibrium, purification, and effluent treatment.

The laboratory develops applied research aimed at process optimization, characterization of adsorbent materials, and performance evaluation of separation systems, contributing to technological innovation and the training of human resources in Chemical Engineering.
